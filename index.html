North Bank Bistro

Farm Fresh Homestyle

Nathan and Samantha Lewis opened North Bank Bistro in the Spring of 2015 in downtown Vancouver, with the help of family and friends. The husband/wife team found inspiration for the restaurant in the farming communities of Washington and Oregon. The contemporary-American menu emphasizes the seasonality of the Northwest.
